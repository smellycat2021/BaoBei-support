Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite a family member?

Open your baby's profile, tap the family sharing icon, and share the invitation link. Family members can join with any role — Parent, Caregiver, or Viewer.

My data isn't syncing across devices. What should I do?

Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID on all devices and that iCloud is enabled. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud and ensure BaoBei is toggled on. Then open the app and pull down to refresh.

How do I delete a baby profile?

Go to Settings within the app, select the baby profile, and choose Delete Profile. This will remove all associated moments, photos, and growth data.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find BaoBei, and tap Cancel Subscription.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings within the app and tap Delete Account. This will remove your personal data and sign you out. Baby profiles you own will also be deleted.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device and synced to your personal iCloud account. We do not operate our own servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What happens to my postcard order?

Postcards are printed and mailed by Lob, our printing partner. Once an order is placed, delivery typically takes 5–10 business days within the US. If you have issues with a specific order, contact us with your order details and we'll help resolve it.
